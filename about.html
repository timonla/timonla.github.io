Short CV

Since spring 2025 I am fully focusing on my own products, which are mostly related to cycling and making music. My main focus these days is helping people share the stories behind their amazing adventures and fitness achievements with Kudoro. You can find out more about what I am up to at klippros.com.

Until spring 2025 I worked as a full stack developer for Manta Marine, where we aimed to save the planet one ship at a time. Previously I worked as an application developer for the Volvo Group.

I got my Msc. in Computer Science at the Chalmers University of Technology in Gothenburg, with a thesis about invariant inference from tests in Boogie. Before that I got my Bsc. in Computer Science at Humboldt University of Berlin with a thesis about the formal verification of heap algorithms using Frama-C.

In April 2019 my brother and I launched a browser extension for a German Counter Strike league platform. We provide seamlessly integrated statistics that save our 3000+ users hours upon hours of their precious time.

I am originally from Berlin, but have my home base in Gothenburg since 2016. These days I am full time traveling around Europe in my van, enjoying the best cycling locations.

Among my other interests are minimalism, bio-hacking, self improvement, veganism, writing music, which I occasionally publish and cooking. You can find my music on my music page.
